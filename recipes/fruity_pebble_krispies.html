Fruity Pebble Krispie Treats

If you love fruity pebbles and rice krispie treats, then this is a quick and easy recipe to give you the best of both worlds. This recipe only takes about 15 minutes to make and provides about 12 tasty treats for you to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons margarine or butter
  • 1 10 ounce package of regular marshmallows or 4 cups mini marshmallows
  • 6 Cups fruity pebbles cereal
