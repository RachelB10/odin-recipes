Fruity Pebble Krispie Treats

If you love fruity pebbles and rice krispie treats, then this is a quick and easy recipe to give you the best of both worlds. This recipe only takes about 15 minutes to make or a half hour if you count waiting time. This resipe also makes about 12 tasty treats for you and others to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons margarine or butter
  • 1 10 ounce package of regular marshmallows or 4 cups mini marshmallows
  • 6 Cups fruity pebbles cereal

Steps

  1. Melt margarine or butter in a large saucepan over low heat.

  2. Add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted, then remove from heat.

  3. Add the fruity pebbles then stir untill they're all well coated and mixed with the melted marshmallows.

  4. Use cooking sprey or wax paper to coat a 13x9x2 inch pan. Then use a buttered spatula or wax paper to evenly press the fruity pebble mixture into the pan and set aside to cool.

  5. Cut the treats into 2-inch squares and enjoy.
