Lemon Pepper Chicken with Onions and Potatos

This is a recipe that my mom would make when I was growing up and that I've always loved. The chicken was always so nice and juicy, the potatos were always so soft and the onions would add good flavor to every bite. I would say this is one of my top favorite foods growing up.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 Boneless chicken breasts
  • 4 Potatos
  • 1 Onion
  • 1-2 Sticks of butter
  • Lemon pepper seasoning
